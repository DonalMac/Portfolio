About me

My name is Donal MacDiarmid.
I have just completed an intensive 6 month retraining course in Full Stack Web Development, with CareerFoundry.
I am originally from the UK but have been living in Fürth just outside Nürnberg for 10 years.
I have a 2:1 Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Computing & an Associate Bachelors Degree in Computing (Pass with Commendation) from the University of Ulster.

I’ve always been passionate about web development. With my refreshed web development skills, I’m looking to start my IT career & showcase my work ethic, character & design capabilities.
